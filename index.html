Objectives:

Your objective is to score as many points as possible by whacking moles that pop up from their holes. Each successful hit on a mole will earn you 5 points.

Avoid Hitting Rabbits:

Avoid clicking on rabbits! If you hit a rabbit, your score will be penalized, and you will lose the game. The game will continue for a set duration ( 30 seconds).

Whack as many moles as you can while avoiding rabbits to achieve the highest score possible.

Hit the reset button to restart the game at any point and the play/stop music button to toggle the game's music. If you need a refresh on the rules, then click the instructions button.

Tips:

Stay focused and be quick with your mouse clicks to whack the moles before they disappear.
Be patient and avoid hasty clicks, especially when a rabbit appears. It's better to miss a mole than to hit a rabbit and lose points.
